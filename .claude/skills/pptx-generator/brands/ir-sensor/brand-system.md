# Brand System — IR-Sensor Projekt

## Style
Clean, professional business presentation. White background, blue accents, clear typography. Suitable for internal management presentations.

## Colors
- Primary: White (#FFFFFF) background
- Text: Dark navy (#1A1A2E)
- Secondary text: Muted blue-gray (#4A4A68)
- Accent: Royal blue (#2563EB)
- Cards: Light gray-blue (#F8FAFC)

## Typography
- Heading: Calibri (bold, large)
- Body: Calibri (regular)
- Code/Technical: Consolas
